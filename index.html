Who I am

I am a software developer who has never been happier where he is than here and now. You're reading this and hoping that you see something interesting in me. I am doing the same as I type this. I'd rather be building something, with a keyboard or with my hands. I build dioramas for fun. They are often nature-themed, or have a kaiju or two stomping around.

I love learning new ways to fix things. I learn fast, I adapt faster, and I find ways to get results as efficiently as possible.

As a backend & frontend developer, I have had the most enlightening experience learning and applying Javascript, REACT/REDUX, Python, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL, Express, Node.js to my suite of projects. I am currently refreshing myself on Java, for which I took classes a number of years ago. I am constantly rebuilding myself, and like how I ofte find something new in my code, I discover something new about myself.

LairBnB





LairBnB is a full stack application for fledgling misunderstood malcontents to getaway and relax. The application is made with a REACT/REDUX template engine and backed by a Postgres database. The React rendering process involves the React source code being compiled into a Javascript function and returns a string of HTML rendered with our implemented LairBnB data. Styling was handled using CSS in JavaScript.

Gora is a full stack horror knowledge sharing application for horror entertainment enthusiasts. Users can satiate their morbid curiousity by asking questions and getting responses from fellow fans of nightmarish media!
